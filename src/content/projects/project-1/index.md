A personal project born out of the frustration of figuring out what to eat every week.

The Batch Cooking App lets you plan your weekly meals, automatically generates a consolidated shopping list, and helps you organize your cooking sessions so you spend less time in the kitchen and more time eating well.

Built with a Python/FastAPI backend, PostgreSQL database, and deployed with Docker.
